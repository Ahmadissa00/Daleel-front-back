Daleel

Your Intelligent Legal Assistant for Jordanian Laws

Daleel is a tech project that provides an intelligent legal assistant designed to answer citizens' questions about Jordanian laws quickly and reliably using Vertex AI. Our mission is to make legal knowledge more accessible and easier to understand for everyone in Jordan.

About Daleel

Daleel addresses the challenge of limited access to accurate legal information for ordinary people. By offering instant legal consultations, we empower citizens with knowledge about their rights and responsibilities under Jordanian law.

Our Solution

Powered by Vertex AI combined with Java/Spring Boot on the backend and modern web technologies on the frontend, Daleel ensures both intelligent processing and a smooth user experience.

Impact

Daleel's impact extends beyond individuals, as it empowers citizens, supports justice, and has the potential to scale to serve institutions and organizations across Jordan.
